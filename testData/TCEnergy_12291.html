UPDATED CAPACITY REDUCTION Wisconsin - Marshfield Viking (Updated: 4/7/25)

Since the last posting, ANR has revised the start date for the planned pipeline maintenance in Wisconsin. ANR anticipates this posting may result in Firm Primary, Firm Secondary and Interruptible curtailments. As a result, capacity will be reduced as follows:

4/14 - 7/31 (Previously 4/9 - 7/31) Marshfield/Viking INT (LOC #28873) ANR will limit net Receipts to 160 MMcf/d

No physical deliveries will be allowed during the planned maintenance. ANR may net delivery nominations against receipt nominations provided the net receipt is in excess of 30 MMcf/d.

Estimated impact to Firm Primary as follows:
0% - Based on the Current Net Shipper Nominations
100% - Based on the Current Contracted MDQ

4/14 - 7/31 (Previously 4/9 - 7/31) Weyauwega Westbound (LOC #505618) 107 MMcf/d (leaving 109 MMcf/d)

Estimated impact to Firm Primary as follows:
0% - Based on the Current Net Shipper Nominations
50% - Based on the Current Contracted MDQ


Any reservation charge credits (RCCs) will be determined per the process set forth in the General Terms and Conditions, Section 6.36 of ANR's FERC Gas Tariff ("Tariff"). However, ANR will only issue RCCs if ANR is unable to schedule or deliver primary firm nominated capacity as a result of the outage. In the event RCCs are issued as set forth in the Tariff, shippers will receive RCCs based on its 7-day Non-Force Majeure Average Usage Quantity, as defined in Section 6.36.2 of ANR's Tariff, less any scheduled quantities on the contract.
